DriveWise is an innovative online platform designed to teach young adults and individuals interested in learning how to drive, offering comprehensive resources and interactive tools to make the process accessible and engaging. Founded by Cheng Hao Jee and developed by the DriveWise development team, which consists of students of Asia Pacific University Kuala Lumpur, including Jeremy Edwin Ephraim, Wong Wei Han, Siw Yong Sheng, Tausif, and led by the founder, Cheng Hao Jee, this project highlights the collaborative efforts of a dedicated group of tech-savvy individuals from Kuala Lumpur's premier technology university.

Officially launched at 8:00 AM on December 17, 2024, the idea for this transformative website was conceived on September 24, 2024, by the founder. DriveWise is not just a platform; it's a vision realized to provide driving education to the digital age. The website caters to a diverse user base, enabling seamless registration and login options for students, lecturers, and administrators.

The platform's functionalities are thoughtfully designed to address the needs of its users: students can take advantage of an interactive quiz page to test and enhance their driving knowledge, while lecturers and administrators are empowered with tools to edit quizzes and manage the article page, ensuring content stays up-to-date and relevant. Everyone, regardless of their role, has access to profile pages where they can view their information and track their progress.

The view article page serves as a valuable resource for all users, providing well-curated content about driving rules, safety tips, and more. DriveWise also includes a video learning page, enhancing the learning experience through multimedia tutorials, and a contact page to ensure open communication between users and the DriveWise team. Students, lecturers, and administrators alike can access their quiz results page, fostering transparency and progress tracking. With its intuitive design, user-focused features, and commitment to education, DriveWise is poised to revolutionize how driving skills are taught and learned, creating a safe and informed community of drivers.
